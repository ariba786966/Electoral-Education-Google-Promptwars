The Electoral Process

Phase 1
Notification: ECI drops the schedule. Game on.
Phase 2
Nomination: Candidates file their papers to run.
Phase 3
Scrutiny & Withdrawal: Checking forms, last chance to drop out.
Phase 4
Campaigning: Rallies, manifestos, the hype train.
Phase 5
Polling Day: Go cast your vote! Need EPIC or valid ID.
Phase 6
Counting: The results are out.

Stay Safe, Be Responsible 🛡️

  • Fact Check: Don't believe everything on WhatsApp. Verify news with official ECI sources.
  • Spam Calls/Texts: Ignore unverified links asking for "voter registration fees" (it's free!).
  • Physical Safety: Be aware of your surroundings on polling day. Report suspicious activity or riots to local authorities.
  • Civic Duty: Do not accept cash, gifts, or liquor for your vote. It's illegal and bad vibes.
  • Data Security: Never share your Aadhaar OTP or Epic Card details with strangers online.
